About Me

Hello! I'm Rukiya Rita, an Assistant English Teacher at Phakal Police Lines School & College, Lalmonirhat. With a strong academic background in English Literature and Linguistics, I am dedicated to fostering a love for language and learning in my students.

My teaching philosophy centers on creating an engaging and interactive classroom environment where students feel empowered to explore, question, and express themselves. I believe in tailoring teaching methods to suit diverse learning styles, ensuring every student has the opportunity to thrive.

I believe education is a collaborative journey where the teacher acts as a facilitator rather than a mere transmitter of knowledge. My approach emphasizes critical thinking, creativity, and real‑world application, ensuring students not only learn English but also develop the confidence to use it effectively.
